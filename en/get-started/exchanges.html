Veil is available on the following exchanges (listed in chronological order):

  • TradeOgre - the most liquidity, all genuine. Cheap and fast withdrawals
  • BitMart - is the world's largest exchange for some coins, so there is huge potential volume, deposits and withdrawals have resumed (2022-05-05), trading against USDT only.
    Withdrawals are INSTANT and only cost 0.2 Veil!
  • Graviex - genuine, but lower volume, instant & cheap withdrawals. Has Veil markets against BTC, ETH & USDT.
  • Crex24 - trading against BTC, but does not operate in the USA, UK, Canada, Australia, New Zealand, Israel, South Korea, China, UAE, Singapore, Hong Kong, Japan, Switzerland, Norway, Russian Federation, Republic of Belarus, Kazakhstan and the European Union.
  • InstaSwap - Veil is listed here, but performance may depend on more available liquidity on other exchanges. Market paired with: BTC, ETH, & LTC.
  • STEX - solid exchange, with potential for more volume, BUT as of May 2022 the STEX market is closed. Please withdraw Veil from STEX until further notice.
  • Probit - an established Korean exchange, still with sporadic Veil volume. Korean regulations make continued listing uncertain. Currently Veil withdrawals on Probit exchange cost 0.2 Veil. Deposits are available and confirm after 30 minutes.
  • New Capital Exchange - Veil's listing is in progress with New Capital Exchange, a small Lithuanian exchange providing a connection with European banking. Further details will be published when this listing goes live.

Note — The exchanges currently accept deposits in Basecoin only, and the Veil wallet currently will only send to stealth addresses on the Send screen. Sending Basecoin from the wallet can be done through the Console, as described in this support article.
