Built with long-term privacy in mind

Veil prioritizes protocol-level privacy, decentralized participation, and conservative engineering decisions over short-term hype or marketing claims.

  • Privacy features are implemented at the protocol level, not as add-ons
  • Mining and staking remain accessible across CPUs and GPUs
  • Development focuses on stability, correctness, and security
  • No reliance on endorsements, paid promotion, or speculative promises
